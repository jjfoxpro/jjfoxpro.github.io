Brainlab® · AANS · ASTRO National quality improvement

SRS Registry

The SRS Registry is a national quality-improvement registry for stereotactic radiosurgery, developed through collaboration between Brainlab, the American Association of Neurological Surgeons (AANS), and the American Society for Radiation Oncology (ASTRO). It brings longitudinal, real-world treatment and outcomes data together to support better care and patient outcomes—and remains in active use.

  • Partner management
  • Clinical registry strategy
  • Program leadership

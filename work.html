Brainlab® · AANS · ASTRO National quality improvement · Active

SRS Registry

A national quality-improvement registry for stereotactic radiosurgery, developed through collaboration between Brainlab, the American Association of Neurological Surgeons (AANS), and the American Society for Radiation Oncology (ASTRO). The registry brings longitudinal, real-world treatment and outcomes data together to support better care and patient outcomes.

The program connected clinical societies, technology teams, and participating centers through shared requirements, coordinated delivery, and sustainable registry operations. It remains in active use.

Bringing clinical societies, technology teams, and participating centers together around shared quality goals.
